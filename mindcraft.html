Installation

How do I install the app?
MindCraft is available for both iOS and Android devices. During your baseline appointment, the research team will provide you with a unique study ID. You can download the app in advance of your baseline appointment or during the baseline appointment, as you prefer:

  • iPhone Users: Download the app from the Apple App store at https://bit.ly/4iKv3LT
  • Android Users: While we are working to have the App on the Play store, you can install the app directly via the apk file from https://bit.ly/3FoVusg. Please follow the below instructions at https://tinyurl.com/mcapkinstall to install the app via the apk file.

If you are reading this from your computer, you can scan the QR codes below to access the download links directly on your phone:

Android

How do I setup the app for the first time?
Setting up the app is very easy. Once you have downloaded the app following the link above, you just need to enter the study ID the research team will provide you on the day of the baseline appointment. The reseach team can assist you with installation, if you need help:

  • When you launch the app for the first time, you must enter the Study ID you received. Please remember that the ID is case-sensitive. You should use only your ID, and anID can be used only once.
  • After entering the ID, read and accept the privacy policy.
  • Allow the app to collect data from your phone. You can disable the options you do not want the app to collect. Depending on the options you allow, your phone will want you to confirm them. Kindly follow the instructions on your phone to give permissions to the app.
